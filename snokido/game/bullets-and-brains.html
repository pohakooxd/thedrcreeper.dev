    Bullets & Brains

    Author : Ajvar Studio  -   6 321 plays

    Bullets & Brains is an arcade shooter set in a post-apocalyptic world filled with hordes of ferocious, rabid zombies hungry for human brains. Play as Miles Tucker or Samara Jones and fight the undead in mission mode or survival mode. In the mission mode you will have to explore the gigantic city to find and destroy the nests making the zombies appear. In survival mode you will have to eliminate as many undead as possible while staying alive in order to obtain the best score. Improve your score and get better reviews based on your progress. Bullets & Brains can also be played with a gamepad!
