    Rocket Bot Royale

    Author : Winterpixel Games  -   133 958 plays

    In the game Rocket Bot Royale you will be able to take part in intense multiplayer tank battles in which you will have to try to survive as long as possible. After being parachuted on a sinking island you will have to annihilate your opponents with your rockets while protecting yourself from their shots. Aim your cannon at the ground and fire to make your tank jump and reach high places. Flee the rising waters so as not to drown, dig tunnels to protect yourself and collect various bonuses on the map that will give you decisive advantages during the game. Rocket Bot Royale is an easy game to learn but offers a technical gameplay that will take time to master. Only the best players will manage to be the last surviving tank! The game offers an online ranking, competitive seasons, a store with many cosmetic items, goals to achieve that will give you rewards, and the possibility to play with your friends in the same games.
