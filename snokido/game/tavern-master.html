    Tavern Master

    Author : Untitled Studio  -   31 241 plays

    In the game Tavern Master you will have to organize and manage your medieval tavern to make it a renowned establishment and attract more and more customers. Buy tables and chairs and have your first customers, hire a waiter, a bartender and a cook to serve all these people. The decorations will brighten up your establishment, the various drinks served at the bar as well as the variety of the cuisine menu will help to bring more and more people to your tavern. Your employees will be able to level up and become more efficient, but be careful to always keep some gold in reserve to pay salaries and buy back raw materials. Complete the missions entrusted to you to progress and thus be able to expand your tavern. You will start with a small establishment but it will be possible to expand your building to make it a tavern that can welcome dozens of customers at the same time.
